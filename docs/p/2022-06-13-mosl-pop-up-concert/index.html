Metropolitan Orchestra of St. Louis: Pop-Up Concert

Jun 13, 2022

One of our lodge members, Ann Dolan, is a flutist in The Metropolitan Orchestra of St. Louis so we visited on of their summer “Pop-Up Concerts” on June 5th. It was an absolutely blissful evening and very entertaining to listen to a concert from a few feet away.
