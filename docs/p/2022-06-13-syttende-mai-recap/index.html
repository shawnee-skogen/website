2022 May Meeting Recap: Syttende Mai

Jun 13, 2022

Gratulerer med dagen! The weather gave us a reprieve and we were able to enjoy a wonderful picnic with our many of our lodge members. In Norwegian tradition hot dogs were grilled and we attempted to serve ice cream. There was a wonderful variety of dishes, some of which were made possible thanks to the Charmans' RV kitchenette. We got to see several lodge members we have not gotten to see in a while!

The festivities took place at Giant City State Park where we had this nice shelter to give us some shade,
